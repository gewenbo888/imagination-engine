# Imagination Engine · 想象力引擎

A massive, bilingual (EN · 中文) exploration of imagination as the brain's engine for **simulating realities** — recombining memory, predicting futures, generating symbols, and modelling worlds before they physically exist. It traces imagination from its evolutionary origin in animal prediction, through the predictive brain, dreams and hallucination, shared myth and money, scientific invention, art, childhood play, digital worlds, and generative AI, to the future of civilisation-scale imagination networks.

The central thesis: **humanity became powerful partly because humans could mentally simulate realities before they physically existed.** Every invention, myth, religion, empire, scientific theory and work of art first existed inside imagination.

## Features

- **Live EN | 中文 toggle** — every heading, paragraph, diagram label and interactive switches language with no reload (React context).
- **Ten systems** — Origin Engine, Brain Simulation, Dreams & Hallucination, Fiction & Myth, Science & Invention, Art & Symbolic Creation, Childhood Imagination, Digital Imagination, AI & Synthetic Imagination, Future Possibility.
- **Custom visualisations** — a Three.js GLSL dream-fluid hero, a deep-time emergence timeline, an interactive predictive-brain map, a consciousness "reality dial", a shared-fiction strata stack, an imagined→built lag chart, a human-vs-AI comparator, a future-possibility horizon, and a seven-axis "Imagination Power" radar profiling seven kinds of mind.
- **Recursive simulation console** — pick a seed (fire, a bird, death, a number…) and watch the same imaginative machinery refract it through memory, prediction, story, invention, myth, art and synthetic imagination.
